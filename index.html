Who We Are

Empower the Homeless is a community initiative in Sydney that uses technology to connect homeless individuals to essential services and provide IT training to improve their future prospects.

Services We Offer

Shelter Directory

We connect individuals with nearby shelters and provide emergency housing options.

Employment Opportunities

Find job listings, employment services, and skill-building workshops.

Healthcare

Access healthcare services, mental health support, and free consultations.

IT Training Program

Our free IT training program offers essential digital literacy skills, job search training, and more. Enroll today to start building your future.
